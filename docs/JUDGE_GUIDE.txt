IDENTITYLENS AI — GUIDE FOR JUDGES
=====================================

What is this?

IdentityLens AI is a platform that helps security teams answer one critical question:
"Who in our organization can cause the most damage if their account is compromised?"

Most companies manage identities across many systems — corporate login, cloud accounts,
HR tools, and more. Each system shows a different piece of the picture. A person might
look low-risk in one place and extremely dangerous in another. IdentityLens brings
everything together into a single view and ranks people by real-world risk.


THE PROBLEM WE SOLVED
---------------------

Security teams today face three painful gaps:

1. BLIND SPOTS
   An employee may appear normal in the HR system but hold admin access in the cloud
   that hasn't been used in months. Traditional tools don't connect those dots.

2. NO PRIORITIZATION
   When everything is flagged as "high priority," nothing gets fixed. Teams need a
   clear ranked list of who to investigate first.

3. NO BUSINESS IMPACT
   Risk reports talk about permissions and roles, but executives need to hear:
   "If this account is breached, 2.4 million customer records could be exposed."

IdentityLens closes all three gaps.


WHAT THE PLATFORM DOES
----------------------

Think of IdentityLens as a risk intelligence command center for identities.

It pulls account information from six platforms (Active Directory, AWS, Azure, Okta,
Salesforce, and ServiceNow) and resolves them into one person — one identity, one
risk score, one story.

From there it:

  • Ranks every identity from 0 to 100 by risk
  • Explains WHY each person scored high (dormant admin access, old API keys,
    terminated employees still active, etc.)
  • Shows what data they could reach if compromised
  • Recommends specific fixes (remove admin access, rotate tokens, disable accounts)
  • Maps findings to compliance frameworks (NIST, MITRE ATT&CK, GDPR, CIS)
  • Uses machine learning to spot unusual behavior patterns

WHY THIS MATTERS
----------------

Identity is the #1 attack vector in enterprise breaches. Attackers don't break in
through firewalls — they log in with valid credentials.

IdentityLens gives security teams:
  • A single pane of glass across fragmented identity systems
  • Quantified, explainable risk — not just alerts, but reasons and impact
  • Actionable remediation — not "investigate further," but "remove AWS admin"
  • Executive-ready reporting — numbers that boards and CISOs understand

For a 350-person organization in this demo, the platform surfaces 14 critical
identities, 4.8 million records at potential exposure, and 5 findings that need
immediate attention — in seconds, not weeks of manual audit work.
